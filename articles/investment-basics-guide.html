Model Portfolios for US Investors

US asset allocation strategies for different risk profiles and time horizons.

Conservative Portfolio (0-3 years)

Focus on capital preservation and liquidity for short-term goals.

  • 50% high-yield savings (4.5-5.2%)
  • 30% short-term Treasury bonds
  • 20% money market funds

Balanced Portfolio (3-7 years)

Mix of US stocks and bonds for moderate growth.

  • 15% cash and savings
  • 45% US large-cap stocks (S&P 500)
  • 25% US bonds (Treasury/Corporate)
  • 15% international stocks

Growth Portfolio (7+ years)

Higher allocation to stocks for long-term wealth building.

  • 75% US stocks (broad market)
  • 15% small-cap growth stocks
  • 10% Treasury bonds for rebalancing
