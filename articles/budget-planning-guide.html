Why Budgeting Matters

Benefits of Budgeting

Reduces financial stress and anxiety

Knowing exactly where your money is going removes uncertainty and gives you peace of mind.

Helps you reach financial goals faster

A clear plan lets you prioritize savings and debt payoff without guesswork.

Prevents overspending and debt accumulation

Popular Budgeting Methods

50/30/20 Rule

Allocate 50% to needs, 30% to wants, and 20% to savings and debt payoff.

Zero-Based Budget

Assign every dollar a job so income minus expenses equals zero.

Envelope Method

Use physical or digital envelopes to control spending in each category.
