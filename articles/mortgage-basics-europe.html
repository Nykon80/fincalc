Mortgage Basics in Europe

January 15, 2024 6 min read

Mortgage structures vary by country, but fixed-rate loans are common.

European Mortgage Types

  • Fixed-Rate: Interest rate remains constant throughout the loan term
  • Variable-Rate: Interest rate can change based on market conditions
  • Tracker: Interest rate follows a specific benchmark (e.g., ECB rate)

Key Considerations

Consider taxes, insurance, and total monthly affordability.

Regional Differences

  • Germany: Long-term fixed rates (10-30 years)
  • France: Flexible repayment options
  • Poland: Growing market with competitive rates
