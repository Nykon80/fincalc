What Is ROI and How To Use It

January 15, 2024 5 min read

ROI measures the efficiency of an investment by comparing the gain or loss relative to the cost.

ROI Formula

ROI = ((Final Value - Initial Investment) / Initial Investment) × 100

Example Calculation

If you invest $1,000 and it grows to $1,200:

ROI = (($1,200 - $1,000) / $1,000) × 100 = 20%

Annualized ROI

Annualized ROI helps compare investments of different durations.

Beyond the Numbers

Consider risk and costs, not just headline return.
