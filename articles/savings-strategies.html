Savings Strategies That Work

January 15, 2024 7 min read

Automate transfers and increase contributions after pay raises.

The 50/30/20 Rule

  • 50%: Essential expenses (housing, food, utilities)
  • 30%: Discretionary spending (entertainment, shopping)
  • 20%: Savings and debt repayment

Automation is Key

Set up automatic transfers to your savings account on payday. This ensures you save before you spend.

Investment Options

Choose high-yield accounts or low-cost index funds for long-term goals.

Track Your Progress

Track progress with milestones and adjust as needed.
