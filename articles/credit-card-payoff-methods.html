Avalanche vs Snowball: Credit Card Payoff Methods

January 15, 2024 6 min read

The Avalanche Method

Avalanche saves more interest by targeting highest APR first.

  • Pay minimum on all cards
  • Put extra money toward highest interest rate card
  • Mathematically optimal approach
  • Saves the most money in interest

The Snowball Method

Snowball builds momentum by paying smallest balances first.

  • Pay minimum on all cards
  • Put extra money toward smallest balance card
  • Psychological benefits
  • Quick wins build motivation

Which Should You Choose?

Pick the method you will stick with consistently.
